Summary:

Keeping Up With the Kardashians follows the lives of ridiculously rich people in California. We keep up with five sisters and their mom/manager Kris as they navigate being super famous. The show includes family vacations, messy love lives, and a deep dive into how these influencers make an immense amount of money. KUWTK ran for 20 seasons on E! Network and the family has now transitioned into recording their lives for more money on Hulu instead.

Now let's get into my thoughts...

Honestly, the Kardashians are everything that is wrong in this world.. but I love them. They are stupid rich and as someone who cannot even fathom having the amount of money they have, I love watching the show. They live in a world so out of touch with reality and its fascinating to see what is normal to them. Plus, watching the show takes no brain power in any capacity because its just a bunch of white women spending money, crying, and fighting. I have seen every season of Keeping Up and now am actively watching their new show, which is the exact same show with better music and they probably make even more money somehow.
