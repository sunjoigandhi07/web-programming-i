Summary:

Love is Blind is a reality dating show that tests if love is truly blind. For ten days, a bunch of single people from the same city date each other but never actually see one another. They spend hours in these pods talking and connecting and at the end of day ten, couples decide if they want to spend the rest of their lives together and get engaged. After they get engaged, only then are participants able to see the partner they have chosen to marry. Once they are revealed, the couples go on vaction before moving back to the city they live in. The couples begin living together as a test run before they get married at the end of the experiment. The entire show is filmed in 38 days, meaning these people meet and decide if they want to get married only knowing one another for slightly over a month.

My thoughts?!??!

This show is nuts. Like what..?? Getting married after only knowing each other for like a month and not even seeing each other until the end of week 2 of talking. And getting engaged before you even physically meet someone? No thanks. I do however, love how messy this show. Though not as messy as recent reality dating series, this show is seriously broken. There is always jealously, resentment, and honestly a surprising amount of love lol.

Season 1 had a lot more success than season 2 but season 2 is WAY more interesting and intriguing than season 1. Plus, the cast in season 2 is way more diverse. Out of all the reality dating tv I watch, which is clearly too much, season 2 of Love is Blind has the best representation I've seen. Now let's rank the couples who got married at the end of the experiment-

Got Married 1. Cameron and Lauren
2. Barnett and Amber
3. Jarrett and Iyanna
4. Nick and Danielle
Hmmm... though 4 couples have gotten married in the 2 seasons, only 2 couples remain together. Cameron and Lauren and Barnett and Amber are both season 1 couples and they are the only two couples that are still together. All season 2 couples broke up and have gotten with other cast members or other people. Honestly, season 1 was not as entertaining as season 2 so I am going to focus on that.
