Summary:

The Real Housewives of Dubai follows the lives of 6 insanely rich women living in Dubai. We see their family lives, professional lives, and personal lives. Most of the time, they are fighting about something ridicuous and there is lots of yelling, crying, and drinking. They wear fabulous outfits and spend most of their time throwing parties for their friends to attend.
